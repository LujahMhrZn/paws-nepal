Terms and Conditions

Last Updated: June 28, 2023

1. Adoption Process

1.1 Eligibility

  • Adopters must be at least 18 years old.
  • All household members must consent to the adoption.

1.2 Application

  • Submission of an application does not guarantee approval.
  • Paws Nepal reserves the right to reject applications.

1.3 Home Checks

We may conduct virtual or in-person home visits to ensure suitability.

2. Dog Care Requirements

2.1 Basic Needs

  • Provide adequate food, water, shelter, and veterinary care.
  • Dogs must not be left chained for extended periods.

2.2 Medical Care

  • Adopters must maintain vaccinations.
  • Immediate veterinary attention required for sick/injured dogs.

3. Fees & Donations

Adoption fees cover vaccinations, microchipping, and sterilization. Donations are non-refundable.

4. Returns & Refunds

If unable to care for the dog, adopters must contact Paws Nepal. No cash refunds after 7 days.

5. Liability

Paws Nepal is not liable for damages caused by adopted dogs. Adopters assume all risks.

6. Privacy Policy

Personal information is used solely for adoption processing. We do not sell your data.

7. Governing Law

These terms are governed by Nepali law. Disputes will be resolved in Kathmandu courts.
