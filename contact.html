Frequently Asked Questions

What is the adoption process?

Our adoption process involves four simple steps: application, home visit, meet & greet, and finalizing adoption. The entire process typically takes 1-2 weeks.

Are all dogs vaccinated?

Yes, all dogs in our care receive complete vaccinations, deworming, and are spayed/neutered before adoption.

Can I return a dog if it doesn't work out?

While we hope every adoption is successful, we understand sometimes it doesn't work out. We have a no-questions-asked return policy within the first month.

Do you offer post-adoption support?

Absolutely! We provide lifetime support for all our adopted dogs, including behavioral advice and discounted veterinary services at our partner clinics.
